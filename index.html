From Observer to Founder: My Journey

The foundation of this club at our school, guided by our history teacher, marked a pivotal moment. Initially, I was simply an observer. As it was the first time such a club was established, I watched from the sidelines, not participating.

The following year, I was eager to move from observer to participant. I joined the club and immediately gained valuable experience, particularly when I had the opportunity to attend and participate in the African Union Assembly. This exposure profoundly built my confidence, honed my diplomatic skills, and solidified my understanding of leadership.

By Grade 12, the trust and skills I had developed led me to be elected President of the club. This position further boosted my confidence, refined my organizational capabilities, and was a profound period of self-discovery.

Upon graduating, I didn't want this journey to end. To continue cultivating leaders, I founded the high level club known as MAU Impact Hub. Here, we discuss crucial ideas, issues, and topics that truly matter, fostering a culture of peace, ethical influence, and proactive leadership. This is how the club began, and how it continues to inspire a new generation.

Our Elite Programs

The Diplomatic Track

Core MAU Simulation & Global Affairs

This rigorous program simulates the workings of the African Union. Students step into the shoes of diplomats, researching country policies, drafting resolutions, and debating critical continental issues. It focuses on public speaking, conflict resolution, and understanding the complex machinery of international relations.

Key Highlights:

  • Weekly Guided Debate Sessions
  • Resolution Drafting Workshops
  • Annual Inter-School MAU Conference

The Impact Accelerator

Leadership & Community Action

Moving beyond theory, this track empowers members to launch real-world community service projects. We provide project management training, mentorship from industry leaders, and resources to turn ideas into tangible social impact. From environmental cleanups to educational tutoring, we bring diplomacy to the streets.

Key Highlights:

  • Project Management Certification
  • Community Service Hours
  • Social Impact Funding Access
